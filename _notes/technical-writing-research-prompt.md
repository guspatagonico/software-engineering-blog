# Prompt base: @technical-writing-research

Objective: Draft a long-form post about [TOPIC] focused on harness engineering and agentic development.

Requirements:

- Separate verified facts from hypotheses/conjectures.
- Provide citations with links and dates for factual claims.
- Compare 2-3 sources and note discrepancies.
- Propose at least 3 visuals (Mermaid/C4/draw-io).
- Include tradeoffs, failure modes, and evaluation metrics.
- Conclude with actionable takeaways.

Constraints:

- Keep the tone technical, precise, and evidence-driven.
- If uncertain, state what would verify the claim.
